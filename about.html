Dr. Shaun James

Assistant Professor of Neuroscience • Principal Investigator

Biography

Dr. Shaun James is an Assistant Professor of Neuroscience at Sacred Heart University and Principal Investigator of the Cortical Analysis Lab. His work integrates multimodal imaging, behavioral neuroscience, neurophysiology, and computational analysis to investigate how cortical networks function across development, disease, and behavior.
